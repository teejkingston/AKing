About Me

I was born in London but I grew up in a rough but very fast paced environment, Nigeria. My parents had separated me from siblings and sent me off to live with a step mother in a strange land (2002).

I was enrolled into a public secondary school in Port-Harcourt at 6years old. A broken roof, windowless windows, a few wooden benches and a faint black chalk board.

Things were rough at first, I was tiny compared to the other kids, most of which were teenagers and young adults already. The only light skinned kid amongst hundreds of kids this resulted in constant bullying.

In 2001 GSM made an inroad into Nigeria, slowly more people had access to mobile phones until it was common. The most popular phone brand at the time was Nokia, with only a few network providers to choose from.

I happened to have access to a Nokia twice a week (mainly so I could speak to my family in the U.K.). I made full use off this opportunity on every occasion.

Scouring the World Wide Web on my borrowed Nokia at the speed of 2g, I stumbled across a forum that described in a step-by-step manner how to manually configure GPRS to access free internet. This was the canon event, that lead me down the tech rabbit hole. At roughly the age 8, I was earning passive income from class/school mates to manually configure their GSM’s to access free internet and the bullies let me be.

With time, as well as being a member to a lot of underground forums sites and communities, I would go on to create my own mobile websites.

With the help of the once popular Indonesian website builder, I self-taught myself the fundamentals of HTML, CSS and even a little JavaScript.

Since then, I’ve worked on countless of website builds for friends. I am eager to delve into app development and mastering the Python language as I think is this is the next step in my journey.

In my spare time I like to learn and practice my graphic design skills. I often play around with Adobe After effects, Premiere pro, photoshop and illustrator.

I have started teaching myself animation, Cinema4d to be precise. I really enjoy creating and producing my visions.

I strongly believe that with technology I can and will change the world.
